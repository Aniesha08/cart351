ABOUT IMPACT

ImpACT is a project created by two university students, Aniesha Sangarapillai and Zahra Ahmadi. The project is a visualization of the flow of emotions of social media users as they come across various situations online.

"Imp" stands for impression and "ACT" stands for action/reaction. The goal of ImpACT is to make its users become conscious of how they act, react and behave towards social media situations. ImpACT will enable the user to reflect upon their relationship with social media and other users of social media. It makes the user understand that the emotions and reactions that we are putting into social media leaves an impression on its environment as well as the users. The situations one faces on social media leaves an impression on the user's life in how one looks at their personal life.

As each user begins to become conscious of themselves as a social media user, social media will become more reliable and sound as an environment. ImpACT will encourage users to have more healthier relationships between the users and more understanding towards each other.


PARTICIPATE

Your participation keeps Impact alive and strong. Take the quiz to find out about the impression you leave as a social media user and how it influences the space.

Each emotion is associated with a color, as you click through the quiz you will notice some colors popping. Red is defined for anger, purple for fear, green for disgust, orange for carefree, yellow for happiness and blue for sadness. In the end, you will see your final results of the options you have chosen together which will be pulsating. Your most dominant color, is your most dominant impression that you leave on your social media platform.
